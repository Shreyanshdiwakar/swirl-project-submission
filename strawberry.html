Strawberry Ice Cream

  • Home
  • Recipes
  • About

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a blender, puree strawberries with lemon juice and half the sugar.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together cream, milk, remaining sugar, and salt.
  3. Stir in the strawberry puree.
  4. Pour mixture into your ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  5. Transfer to a container and freeze for at least 2 hours, or until firm.
  6. Enjoy your fruity strawberry ice cream!

Fun Fact: Strawberries are the only fruit with seeds on the outside!
