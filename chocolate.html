Chocolate Ice Cream

  • Home
  • Recipes
  • About

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips (melted)
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk together cream, milk, sugar, cocoa powder, melted chocolate, and salt until smooth.
  2. Pour mixture into your ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  3. Transfer to a container and freeze for at least 2 hours, or until firm.
  4. Enjoy your rich and creamy chocolate ice cream!

Fun Fact: Chocolate ice cream was invented before vanilla!
