Bubblegum Ice Cream

  • Home
  • Recipes
  • About

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon bubblegum flavoring
  • Pink or blue food coloring (optional)
  • 1/2 cup chopped bubblegum candies (optional)
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together cream, milk, sugar, bubblegum flavoring, food coloring, and salt until sugar dissolves.
  2. Pour mixture into your ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  3. In the last 2 minutes of churning, add the chopped bubblegum candies if using.
  4. Transfer to a container and freeze for at least 2 hours, or until firm.
  5. Enjoy your fun and colorful bubblegum ice cream!

Fun Fact: Bubblegum ice cream is a favorite at many kids' birthday parties!
