Mint Chip Ice Cream

  • Home
  • Recipes
  • About

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • Green food coloring (optional)
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together cream, milk, sugar, peppermint extract, food coloring, and salt until sugar dissolves.
  2. Pour mixture into your ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  3. In the last 2 minutes of churning, add the chocolate chips.
  4. Transfer to a container and freeze for at least 2 hours, or until firm.
  5. Enjoy your refreshing mint chip ice cream!

Fun Fact: Mint chocolate chip is one of the top 10 most popular ice cream flavors in the U.S.!
